## Release 122 earned absurd outcomes

### Troll restraint

- Alert restraint fails.
- `TRICK TROLL` creates one immediate opportunity.
- Immediate restraint leaves the troll alive, conditionally drops the real axe, commits the real rope, and opens the passages.
- `UNTIE TROLL` restores rope and danger.
- Wasting the opening restores ordinary hostility.

### Burning the nest

- `PUT SACK UNDER TREE` prepares the real sack.
- `BURN NEST WITH TORCH` requires the held, lit ivory torch and real nest.
- Preparation preserves the real egg.
- No preparation invokes canonical `BAD-EGG` and existing broken egg/canary state.
- Ordinary burning delegates to canonical `V-BURN`.
- No treasure or puzzle object is duplicated.
