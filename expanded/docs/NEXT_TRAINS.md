## 1. Absurd Alternate Zork I Release 122 — complete

Release 122 derives from the complete qualified Release 121 source and changes exactly five paths.

### Troll restraint

- alert rope restraint fails;
- `TRICK TROLL` creates one immediate opportunity;
- immediate `TIE UP TROLL WITH ROPE` leaves him alive, drops the axe, consumes the rope into the restraint, and opens the passages;
- `UNTIE TROLL` restores the rope and danger;
- leaving instead of acting wastes the opportunity, and returning proves normal hostile behavior.

### Nest fire

- `PUT SACK UNDER TREE` prepares the real brown sack;
- `BURN NEST WITH TORCH` requires the real held, lit ivory torch and the nest still in the tree;
- prepared catch preserves the real egg;
- unprepared fall invokes canonical `BAD-EGG` and its existing broken egg/canary consequences;
- ordinary burning still uses canonical `V-BURN`;
- no treasure or puzzle object is duplicated.

## 1.1 Further earned alternate solutions — next `.z3` product train after parity priorities

Candidate actions must satisfy all of these:

- use existing visible objects or deliberately added coherent objects;
- require preparation, timing, positioning, or genuine vulnerability;
- preserve original solutions;
- have meaningful failure consequences;
- avoid duplicate treasure or parallel puzzle state;
- remain funny because the physics is ridiculous, not because the parser grants arbitrary wishes.

Potential investigations:

- tricking or restraining another actor only where motives and objects support it;

- using fire, water, rope, containers, or machinery in unusual but material-consistent ways;
